Our Story

Launched in 2024, Innocraft is the premier online platform dedicated to handmade crafts and artisans, with an active presence both locally and globally.

Supported by a wide range of tailored marketing tools, community features, and skill development resources, Innocraft connects over 5,000 artisans and craft makers with buyers who value unique, customizable, and eco-friendly products.

The platform empowers artisans by providing professional profiles, a sustainable marketplace, and opportunities to organize craft workshops. With a growing community of creators and enthusiasts, Innocraft serves thousands of customers while promoting craftsmanship and sustainable.
